# Solutions to Exercise 1

## Marketing Example: Understanding the Customer Journey

### Comprehensive Solution:
In today's marketing landscape, it is crucial to understand the customer journey from awareness to decision. This includes identifying key touchpoints where potential customers interact with your brand. For example, a potential customer might first know about your product through a social media ad, further research the product on your website, and finally decide to make a purchase based on a positive review from a trusted source.

### Key Takeaways:
1. **Awareness**: This is where potential customers first learn about your product. Utilize channels like social media, email marketing, and search engine ads to increase exposure.
2. **Consideration**: Ensure your website is optimized for user experience and provides ample information that answers potential questions.
3. **Decision**: Encourage conversions through customer reviews, testimonials, and special offers that create a sense of urgency.

By understanding these steps, marketing efforts can be tailored to guide customers effectively through their journey, leading to higher conversion rates.
